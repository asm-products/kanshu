Kanshu is an app that makes it possible for you to read advanced Chinese texts, even if you are only a beginner.

It does not matter how advanced the text is or what your level of proficiency is. With Kanshu, everything will feel aligned to how much you actually know.

Read any Chinese content you find online

We offer a rich selection of Chinese newspapers, blogs, and online articles for you to read. But if your favorite newspaper or blog is not listed, why not add them yourself? The app offers you that much flexibility.

Adjust the text to your own level

The app features a slider designed to give you control over how the text is displayed.

From absolute beginner, where the texts are displayed in both Pinyin and Hanzi alongside their meanings, to an advanced HSK6 level, where only the Hanzi are displayed.

You decide!

Learn as you read and interact with others

Come across a Chinese character you didn't know? The app allows you to save it for later review and practice.

You can also like an article or comment on it. Other people can then read your comments, and you can read theirs.

Practice to gain mastery

Master your characters using Kanshu's flashcards and other learning tools. Each is personalized, based on characters you save or add.

And the best part? The app will follow your progress and display the characters you have mastered in advanced mode when you read your next article.

Track your progress in real time.

The Kanshu app keeps a detailed record of your progress, with metrics such as the number of saved characters and the number of mastered characters. You can also see the number of characters from a spacific HSK level that you have mastered.

Your progress is quantified and visualized.
