Reading Chinese should be
this easy

Kanshu is an app that makes it possible for you to read texts in advanced Chinese, even if you are just a beginner and do not know that many Chinese characters.

It does not matter how advanced the text is to your level of proficiency. With Kanshu everything will feel aligned to how much you actually know.

Read any Chinese content you find online

We offer you a rich selection of newspapers, blogs and online articles in Chinese for you to read. Is your favorite newspaper or blog not listed? Why not add them yourself? The app offers you that much flexibility.

Adjust the text to your own level

The app features a slider specially designed to give you control of how the text is displayed to you. From absolute beginner, where the texts are displayed both in Pinyin and Hanzi and also show their meanings, to an advanced HSK6 level, where only the Hanzi are displayed. You decide!

Learn as you read and interact with other readers

Did you come across a Chinese character you didn't know? The app allows you to save it for later, so you may review and practice it.

You can also like an article or comment on it. Others will then be able to view your comments, and so will you be able to see theirs.

Use our exercises to master your saved characters

When you came across that Chinese character you did not know, did you save it?

The app offers flashcards and other memorization exercises. They are all personalized, based on characters you save or add yourself.

And the best part? The app will understand your progress and display the characters you have mastered in advanced mode when you read your next article.

Use the app analytics to see your progress in real time.

The Kanshu app keeps a detailed record of your progress, with metrics such as the number of characters you have saved versus those you have mastered, and the number of characters from a spacific HSK level that you have mastered.

It is your progress quantified and visualized, so that you do not need a teacher to tell you how much you have learned.
